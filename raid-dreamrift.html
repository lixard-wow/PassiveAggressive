The Dreamrift

1 Boss • Opened March 17, 2026 • No trash, no warmup — one prolonged fight from the first pull. Difficulty comparable to a mid-tier Voidspire boss.

Boss 1 of 1 — Only Boss

Chimaerus the Undreamt God

Hazelnuttygames — Chimaerus the Undreamt God

Add control is everything. Every Swarming Shade that reaches Chimaerus triggers Insatiable — the boss heals, gains a stacking damage buff, and deals a massive raid-wide spike. There is no recovering from multiple Insatiable triggers.

🛡 Tank
  • Two groups alternate soaking Alndust Upheaval — the soaking group gains Alnsight (required to damage adds) and is debuffed with Rift Vulnerability (1.5 min, increases next Upheaval damage). Rotate groups strictly and never overlap.
  • Tank the Colossal Horror add — its melee damage increases over time. Rotate defensives and communicate incoming spikes to healers.
  • Position Chimaerus away from the raid at all times — Rending Tear targets a random player with a heavy frontal cone and bleed. Boss facing is critical.
  • Intercept Swarming Shades heading toward the boss and assist DPS in killing them immediately — every Shade that reaches Chimaerus triggers Insatiable.
✚ Healer
  • Consuming Miasma targets several players with a large circle dealing damage every 1.5 seconds for 1 minute — do NOT dispel in place. Players must first move to a designated puddle before being dispelled or the dispel splashes the nearby raid.
  • Caustic Phlegm applies a 12-second raid-wide DoT — maintain consistent throughput throughout; it hits all players simultaneously.
  • Rift Sickness (after each Rift Emergence) applies absorb shields to all players — keep healing output high to break through them.
  • Save a major cooldown for the intermission — Corrupted Devastation deals heavy sustained damage while Chimaerus is immune (Rift Shroud). Split the raid into two groups of 1T / 2H / 7 DPS before the pull.
⚔ DPS
  • Alnsight is required to damage adds — earn it by soaking Alndust Upheaval with your assigned group. Never engage adds without the buff.
  • Swarming Shades are the top kill priority — every one that reaches Chimaerus triggers Insatiable (boss heals, gains a stacking damage buff, raid takes a massive spike). Assign dedicated killers before the pull.
  • Interrupt Haunting Essence casts — both the fear and the bolt attacks are interruptible. Assign a rotation before the pull; do not leave this to chance.
  • During the intermission, Chimaerus takes 99% reduced damage (Rift Shroud) — focus entirely on dodging Corrupted Devastation breath sweeps and avoiding ground puddles. Do not DPS.
🛡 Tank
  • Rift Vulnerability from Alndust Upheaval lasts longer — strict group rotation is mandatory. Any overlap causes near-lethal damage on the next soak.
  • Colossal Horror melee damage ramps faster — rotate defensives more frequently and pre-call spikes to healers earlier.
  • Rending Tear bleed is stronger and persists longer — pre-position the raid away from the boss's facing angle before each cast.
  • Swarming Shade spawn frequency increases — communicate immediately when Shades break through so they are intercepted before reaching Chimaerus.
✚ Healer
  • Consuming Miasma applies to more players simultaneously — coordinate multiple puddle positions before the pull and assign each affected player a specific puddle.
  • Caustic Phlegm ticks more frequently — sustained throughput demand increases noticeably across the fight.
  • Rift Sickness absorb shields are larger — more healing output required between Upheaval soaks to keep the raid functional.
  • Corrupted Devastation during intermission hits harder — a major cooldown is mandatory for this window, not optional. Reserve a second cooldown for the intermission on Heroic.
⚔ DPS
  • Swarming Shade spawn rate increases — tighter kill windows with less margin. Pre-assign dedicated Shade killers and backup killers.
  • Haunting Essence casts more frequently — interrupt rotations must include more players to reliably cover every cast.
  • Insatiable triggers are more punishing on Heroic — even one missed Shade cascades significantly. Treat every wave as zero-tolerance.
  • Alnsight uptime is more critical — losing the buff during a Shade wave means missed kills and Insatiable stacks that compound rapidly.
🛡 Tank
  • Colossal Horror gains a periodic void pulse to nearby players — tank it away from the raid at all times, not just away from the boss.
  • Alndust Upheaval timing becomes less predictable — both soak groups must stay staged and ready at all times, not just the active one.
  • Rending Tear can target the same player twice in quick succession — healers must monitor targeted players more closely and communicate repeats immediately.
  • Communicate every Swarming Shade position update via voice — Mythic spawn patterns require full team awareness to avoid Insatiable.
✚ Healer
  • Consuming Miasma dispel positioning is more time-sensitive — incorrect dispels in later waves can cascade into a wipe. Enforce puddle movement strictly before every dispel.
  • Full cooldown timeline must be planned before the pull — improvised usage on Mythic will result in running dry during intermission or peak Shade waves.
  • Rift Sickness absorb shields overlap with Caustic Phlegm in later waves — assign one healer to absorb-breaking and one to sustained throughput.
⚔ DPS
  • Swarming Shade waves overlap with Haunting Essence spawns — interrupt rotations and Shade kills must be executed simultaneously without confusion.
  • Insatiable stacks are significantly more punishing on Mythic — even a partial Shade wave reaching Chimaerus can cascade into an unrecoverable state.
  • Assign movement-speed abilities and slowing effects on Swarming Shades before the pull — improvised slowing on Mythic is not reliable enough.
