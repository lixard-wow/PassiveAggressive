The Dreamrift

1 Boss • Opened March 17, 2026 • No trash, no warmup — one prolonged fight from the first pull. Difficulty comparable to a mid-tier Voidspire boss.

Boss 1 of 1 — Only Boss

Chimaerus the Undreamt God

The fight is won or lost on add control. Every Manifestation that reaches the boss permanently empowers it. Three stacks and the fight becomes unwinnable.

🛡 Tank
  • Position Chimaerus centrally — Manifestations spawn from the arena's edges and converge inward.
  • Intercept Manifestations entering Reality by standing in their path to briefly slow their advance.
  • Taunt any Manifestation that slips past DPS — prevent it from reaching Chimaerus at all costs.
  • Use defensives after each intermission — the boss's melee damage scales with every Cannibalized Essence stack.
  • Face Chimaerus away from the raid — frontal Phlegm Glob salvos spread in a wide arc.
✚ Healer
  • Rift Sickness deals sustained Nature damage to all players every second — this is a throughput fight, not a reactive one.
  • Heal proactively and maintain steady output throughout — do not wait for health bar spikes.
  • Time cooldowns for when multiple Manifestations are vulnerable simultaneously (peak DPS window = peak incoming damage).
  • After each intermission, top the tank immediately — Chimaerus resets aggro and opens with heavy melee.
  • Split the raid into two equal groups (1 Tank / 2 Healers / 7 DPS each) before the pull for efficient Manifestation coverage.
⚔ DPS
  • You cannot damage Manifestations without Alnsight — gain the buff before engaging adds.
  • Clear Manifestations in strict priority order: the type closest to Chimaerus always dies first.
  • Every Manifestation consumed by Chimaerus adds a permanent +50% damage stack to the boss.
  • After 3–4 Cannibalized Essence stacks the fight becomes mathematically unwinnable — zero tolerance for missed adds.
  • Save AoE burst cooldowns for windows when multiple Manifestations become vulnerable simultaneously.
  • Avoid standing in front of Chimaerus during Phlegm Glob — the cone hits anyone near the frontal arc.
🛡 Tank
  • Chimaerus gains Cannibalized Essence 50% faster — each add must die within 8 seconds of spawn or the spiral begins.
  • Manifestations on Heroic move faster toward the boss — tank interceptions must happen earlier in their path, not near the center.
  • Melee damage after intermissions scales more steeply with each Essence stack — rotate defensives more conservatively.
  • Pre-position for each new wave's entry point — reaction intercepts are too slow on Heroic spawn speeds.
✚ Healer
  • Rift Sickness ticks every 0.75 seconds instead of every 1 second — sustained throughput requirement increases by 25%.
  • Reserve a second major cooldown specifically for the mid-fight intermission — Normal-tier cooldown rotation is insufficient on Heroic.
  • Peak DPS windows for adds on Heroic are shorter — coordinate cooldown timing more precisely with DPS.
  • The raid takes increased damage during AoE add windows — do not drop throughput during these moments even if you're moving.
⚔ DPS
  • A 4th Manifestation type appears on Heroic — the Void Shard — which reflects damage back to any melee attacker. Assign ranged DPS exclusively to Void Shard duty.
  • Void Shards must be called out immediately on spawn — melee DPS must avoid engaging them completely.
  • Three Manifestation types now active simultaneously in later waves — kill priority is: closest to boss first, Void Shard second (ranged only), then furthest.
  • Alnsight uptime is more critical — losing the buff mid-wave on Heroic causes a missed Manifestation that accelerates the loss spiral.
🛡 Tank
  • Dream Collapse at 50% and 25% HP physically shrinks the arena ring. Players outside the new boundary after 3 seconds are killed instantly.
  • Communicate Dream Collapse the moment it begins — the visual indicator is subtle and easy to miss mid-fight.
  • After each collapse, reposition Chimaerus to the new center — the boss must remain at the center of the reduced arena.
  • Post-collapse melee damage spikes significantly due to the reduced space and continued Essence scaling — use defensives immediately after boundary forms.
✚ Healer
  • Dream Collapse locks all healing cooldowns for 15 seconds — your major cooldowns go on extended lockout after each collapse. Plan throughput around these forced dead windows.
  • Pre-top the entire raid before each expected collapse — anyone below 50% HP entering the boundary change is at high risk of dying to the arena damage tick.
  • Two Dream Collapses means two 15-second lockout windows — map your full cooldown roster to the timeline before the pull.
  • Rift Sickness damage does not pause during collapse — maintain throughput even while repositioning in the shrinking arena.
⚔ DPS
  • During Dream Collapse, Manifestations spawn at triple the normal rate for 10 seconds. Pre-assign a 3-person dedicated add kill team that does nothing else during this window.
  • The add kill team must maintain Alnsight through the collapse transition — losing the buff during triple-spawn is an unrecoverable cascade.
  • The reduced arena after each collapse limits movement paths — pre-plan Manifestation intercept angles for the smaller space.
  • Use Heroism/Lust immediately after the first Dream Collapse resolves — the subsequent triple-spawn window plus higher damage environment is the fight's peak pressure point.
