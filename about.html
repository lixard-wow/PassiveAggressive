About the Guild

⚔

Who We Are

PassiveAggressive is a Mythic+ focused guild on US-Area 52. The core group has been pushing keys together since Dragonflight — we're not a new group finding our footing, we're an established crew that decided to make it official. The name fits. We're friendly, a little sarcastic, and genuinely good at the game.

🕑

What We Do

Mythic+ is our main thing. We push score each season, work on routes and comps, and help each other improve. We run the occasional raid for tier pieces and trinkets that make our keys faster — but we're not a raiding guild. Once we have what we need, we're back in dungeons.

🏆

Our Community

Most of our time together happens in Discord — between runs, theorycrafting builds, or just talking. If you want a guild that's actually active and not just a name on your character screen, this is it. Feel free to join our Discord and hang out before you even apply.
