About the Guild

⚔

Who We Are

PassiveAggressive is a tight-knit raiding guild of adults who take the game seriously — just not ourselves. We clear content, we have fun, and we occasionally agree on loot.

🕑

Raid Philosophy

We run a structured two-night progression schedule. We expect preparation, attendance, and a positive attitude. Complaining in raid chat counts as a donation to the repair fund.

🏆

What We Offer

Consistent progression, a drama-free environment, consumables for mains, helpful senior members, and a Discord that's 60% memes and 40% strategy.
