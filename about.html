About the Guild

Who We Are

PassiveAggressive is a Mythic+ focused guild on Area 52 (US). The guild was created by a group of players who have been pushing keys together since Dragonflight. Instead of relying on pugs, we wanted a consistent group of players who enjoy Mythic+ and log in regularly to run keys.

What We Do

Our focus is building a small but reliable community where players can improve each season, push higher keys, and enjoy the game without unnecessary drama. While we occasionally run raids, Mythic+ will always remain the core activity of the guild.

Our Community

PassiveAggressive values consistency, teamwork, and a relaxed environment. The goal is always to enjoy the game while pushing keys together. Feel free to join our Discord and hang out before you even apply.
