About the Guild

⚔

Who We Are

PassiveAggressive is a brand new Mythic+ focused guild on US-Area 52, founded February 25, 2026. We're experienced players who live in the dungeon pool — pushing high keys, timing runs, and helping each other improve every week.

🕑

Our Goals

We're building a roster of reliable, skilled players who want to push keys at a high level. Whether you're chasing score, grinding your best friend's key, or just want a steady group to run with — we're building that environment from the ground up.

🏆

Get In Early

We're just getting started and looking for founding members who want to help set the tone. Good attitude, consistent availability, and a drive to improve is all we ask. Apply through the site or jump in Discord and introduce yourself.
