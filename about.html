About the Guild

⚔

Who We Are

PassiveAggressive is a Mythic+ focused guild on US-Area 52 built around a core group of players who have been pushing keys together since the Dragonflight expansion. While the guild itself was officially formed in 2026, most of our members have been running Mythic+ together long before that. We focus on Mythic+ progression, consistent groups, and improving together each season.

🔑

What We Do

Mythic+ is the heart of PassiveAggressive. We run keys regularly, push seasonal score, and work together to improve routes, coordination, and performance. We run an optional Sunday raid night for tier pieces and trinkets — but once geared, it's back to dungeons. Most guild activity happens in Discord between runs.

👥

Our Community

PassiveAggressive values consistency, teamwork, and a relaxed environment. Sarcasm and playful banter are part of the guild culture, but the goal is always to enjoy the game while pushing keys together. Feel free to join our Discord and hang out before you even apply.
