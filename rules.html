Guild Rules

These rules apply in Discord, in-game, and anywhere else PassiveAggressive members interact. They exist to keep the guild a good place to be. They are not complicated.

No Politics. No Religion.

This is non-negotiable. No debate, no "light" discussion, no exceptions. Keep it out. If you're not sure whether something crosses the line, don't post it.

Be Respectful

We're PassiveAggressive in name only. Treat people well. No harassment, slurs, or toxicity toward guild members, applicants, or anyone else.

No Spam

Don't flood channels. Use the designated channels for bot commands and testing. If there's a channel for it, use that channel.

Stay On Topic

Use the right channels. Memes go in the memes channel. Key talk goes in key channels. Keeping things organized makes it easier for everyone to find what they need.

Follow Discord TOS

No illegal content, no doxxing, nothing that violates Discord's Terms of Service. This should go without saying.

Have Fun

We're here to push keys and enjoy WoW. Keep it friendly, keep it fun. The guild works because people actually like being in it.

Breaking these rules will result in warnings, mutes, or removal depending on severity. We want this to be a good space for everyone — and that means holding the line when it needs to be held.
