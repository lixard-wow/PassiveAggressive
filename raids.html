Mythic+

Mythic+ is what we do. Our group has been pushing keys together since Dragonflight and we're not slowing down. We run all week — if you're on and want to push, someone's usually around.

We typically run keys in the 10–15 range. Players still working their way up are welcome as long as you're willing to learn and put in the effort.

Seasonal Key Pushing

From launch week to the final stretch, we're always pushing score. Every season we set new goals and grind toward them as a group — whether that's hitting 3k, clearing the top keys, or just beating last season's best.

Dungeon Optimization

We care about routes, skips, and class utility. If there is a smarter way to run a dungeon, we figure it out. We share notes in Discord and keep our routes sharp as the meta evolves through the season.

Team Coordination

Consistent groups outperform randoms every time. We coordinate comps, share knowledge, and push each other to improve — whether you're a veteran or still climbing the key ladder.
