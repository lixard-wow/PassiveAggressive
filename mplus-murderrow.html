Algeth'ar Academy

4 Bosses • The Azure Span • A dracthyr academy repurposed for M+. Features echo mechanics, AoE management, add waves, and a final boss DPS race.

⚔ Trash Pull Notes

📶 Priority Targets
  • Void Cultist Invoker — backline caster firing Void Bolt Volley at the whole party. Kill before melee mobs.
  • Row Lieutenant — buffs all nearby allies with Syndicate Tactics (+30% damage). Kill immediately or the entire pull becomes lethal.
  • Silkblade Enforcer — stealths and reappears attacking a random party member. Can be stunned mid-stealth with AoE stuns; kill fast.
  • Void Hound — fixates on the healer. Tank picks up immediately; DPS burns on priority.
⚡ Must Kick
  • Void Bolt Volley (Void Cultist Invoker) — party-wide shadow burst. Top interrupt priority every pull.
  • Syndicate Tactics (Row Lieutenant) — 30% damage increase to all allies in range. Kick or the whole pull goes lethal.
  • Vanishing Poison (Silkblade Rogue) — applies a stacking lethal DoT to a random player. Interrupt every cast.
  • Dark Mending (Void Cultist) — heals the caster and nearby allies. Interrupt before it undoes progress.
🔒 CC Targets
  • Silkblade Rogues — Sap (Rogue, must be out of combat) is the cleanest option. Polymorph or Hex also work.
  • Void Cultist Invokers — Polymorph (humanoid). CC the second one when pulling double-caster packs.
  • Row Lookouts — can be Sapped before combat by a Rogue. Imprison (DH), Ring of Frost (Mage) otherwise.
  • Silkblade Assassins — Polymorph, Imprison, Hex. High CC priority when pulling groups of three or more.
🔮 Purge / Soothe
  • Syndicate Empowerment (Row Lieutenant buff on allies) — Purge (Shaman), Spellsteal (Mage), Devour Magic (Warlock). Strip it off the buffed mobs immediately.
  • Void Frenzy (Void Hound enrage) — Soothe (Druid) or Naturalize (Evoker) removes this before the tank spikes.
Boss 1 of 4

Vexamus

Mana Bombs explode on the 2 marked players — move away from the group before detonation. Overload is the group burst window. Kill Arcane Tendril adds before they cast Overgrowth.

🛡 Tank
  • Position Vexamus away from walls — Arcane Burst knockback can pin the tank against surfaces.
  • Pick up Arcane Tendril adds when they spawn — do not let them reach the group.
  • Defensives for Overload — it spikes heavily on the tank and coincides with add pressure.
✚ Healer
  • Mana Bomb explodes on 2 marked players — top them before detonation. The blast deals significant arcane damage.
  • Overload is the primary group burst window — use a major cooldown before it resolves.
  • Tendril Overgrowth is a persistent root on random players — dispel immediately so they can dodge Mana Bomb.
⚔ DPS
  • Move away from the group when marked with Mana Bomb — the explosion deals AoE damage to nearby players.
  • Kick: Overgrowth from Arcane Tendril adds — prevents the root effect from landing on party members. Rotate 2 DPS on this while the third maintains boss pressure.
  • Kill Arcane Tendril adds quickly — their Overgrowth roots chain and create dangerous movement restrictions.
Boss 2 of 4

Overgrown Ancient

Lumbering Swipe is a wide cleave — stay behind or beside the boss, never in front. Controlled Burn spawns fire patches. Barkbreaker is the tank buster.

🛡 Tank
  • Barkbreaker is the primary tank buster — use a major defensive every time. It applies a physical vulnerability debuff.
  • Face the Overgrown Ancient away from the group at all times — Lumbering Swipe cleaves anyone in the frontal cone.
  • Reposition after each Lumbering Swipe — the boss's facing can shift unexpectedly.
✚ Healer
  • Controlled Burn spawns persistent fire patches — keep the group moving out of them. They do not despawn quickly.
  • Barkbreaker vulnerability debuff plus fire patch damage creates compound pressure on the tank — pre-cooldown.
  • Bark Armor phase reduces boss damage taken significantly — use this window for group healing recovery.
⚔ DPS
  • Stay behind or to the side of the Overgrown Ancient — Lumbering Swipe is a wide frontal and deadly to anyone in the arc.
  • Move out of Controlled Burn fire patches immediately — they persist and stack damage quickly.
  • Kick: Mystic Vapor — the sleeping cloud disorients players and reduces healing received. Rotate all 3 DPS.
Boss 3 of 4

Crawth

A repeat of the Maisara trap logic: use mechanics intentionally. Greatwing Slash is a frontal — tank faces Crawth away. Frenzy is the tank damage spike.

🛡 Tank
  • Face Crawth away from the group — Greatwing Slash cleaves the frontal arc.
  • Frenzy increases Crawth's attack speed significantly — rotate defensives rapidly during Frenzy phases.
  • Soothing Breath adds periodically put players to sleep — pick them up immediately to maintain threat.
✚ Healer
  • Frenzy phases have rapid melee swings — pre-cast strong direct heals on the tank and have a cooldown ready.
  • Sonic Shriek hits the whole group simultaneously — keep everyone above comfortable HP before each cast.
  • Slumbering Plume puts random players to sleep — dispel the sleep effect immediately.
⚔ DPS
  • Stay out of Crawth's frontal arc during Greatwing Slash — the cleave is wide and fast.
  • Kick: Sonic Shriek — uninterrupted it deals group-wide sonic damage and disorients players. Rotate all 3 DPS.
  • Kill Soothing Breath adds quickly — they sleep nearby players and disrupt the fight if left alive.
Boss 4 of 4 — Final

Echo of Doragosa

A DPS race. Arcane Expulsion is the primary wipe condition if uninterrupted. Overwhelming Energy is a soak mechanic — spread to take individual hits. Energy Sphere adds must not reach the boss.

🛡 Tank
  • Overwhelming Energy drops a large void zone — drop it at the arena edge and move away immediately.
  • Pick up Energy Spheres via AoE threat — they walk toward Doragosa and heal her significantly if they reach her.
  • Defensives for Arcane Expulsion damage even when interrupted — the partial cast still deals tank damage.
✚ Healer
  • Overwhelming Energy hits the targeted player and anyone nearby — spread the group to minimize chain damage.
  • Energy Spheres dealing passive group AoE while alive — the more spheres active simultaneously, the higher the background damage.
  • Arcane Expulsion is the fight-defining interrupt. Prepare a major cooldown for any Expulsion that slips through.
⚔ DPS
  • Kick: Arcane Expulsion every cast — this is the primary wipe condition. Rotate all 3 DPS in a set order. Agree assignments before the pull; no one can be locked out.
  • Kill Energy Sphere adds before they reach the boss — each one that connects heals Doragosa significantly.
  • Use Heroism/Lust at the start of the fight — this is a DPS race and front-loading pressure limits the number of Arcane Expulsion casts you have to manage.
