Raid

We run occasional raids mainly for tier pieces and trinkets — gear that makes our Mythic+ runs faster. It's optional and secondary to keys. Once we have what we need, we go back to dungeons.

Day Time Type Focus
Sunday 7:00 PM ST Progression AOTC / BIS Gear for M+
🏆

Why We Raid

Tier sets and trinkets. That's it. We raid to gear up for Mythic+, not because raiding is the goal. We aim for AOTC early in the season, get what we need, and spend the rest of our time where we actually want to be — in dungeons.

⚠

M+ Players First

We're not looking for dedicated raiders. If Mythic+ is your main content and you're okay doing the occasional raid night for gear, you'll fit right in. If you're only interested in raiding and not keys, this probably isn't the right home.

⚔

Stay Flexible

Raid nights move fast and comp needs shift. Being willing to swap specs when needed keeps things smooth for everyone. If you know your class well enough to flex, that's all we ask.
