Our Goal

We raid once a week at the start of each season with a clear objective — get AOTC and farm the BIS gear our members need for high M+. Raiding is a tool, not the endgame. Once we have what we need, we're back in keys.

⚠

Not Looking for Raiders

We are not recruiting dedicated raiders or raid loggers. If you only plan to show up for raid night and disappear otherwise, this isn't the right fit. We want M+ players who are willing to raid, not raiders who tolerate M+.

⚔

Be Flexible

Raid comp needs change week to week. We expect members to be open to swapping specs when needed. Come prepared with knowledge of your off-specs. Flexibility is how we fill the raid and keep things moving.
