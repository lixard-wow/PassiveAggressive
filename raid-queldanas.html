March on Quel'Danas

2 Bosses • Opened March 31, 2026 • The most demanding raid of Season 1. A Light & Void alignment system introduced on Boss 1 carries directly into the final boss — understand it or wipe on the last fight.

Boss 1 of 2

Belo'ren, Child of Al'ar

Hazelnuttygames — Belo'ren, Child of Al'ar

A phoenix raised to guard the Sunwell. Every player is assigned a Light or Void Feather at the start — absorb only your matching color or suffer heavy penalties. The fight is a repeating phoenix-and-egg cycle that gets harder to heal with every resurrection.

🛡 Tank
  • Guardian's Edict: Belo'ren emits a Light cone and a Void cone simultaneously — the Light-assigned tank must absorb the Light cone, the Void-assigned tank absorbs the Void cone. Missing your cone gives Belo'ren a stacking damage buff. This is a wipe condition if it accumulates.
  • Confirm Feather color assignments with your co-tank before the pull and re-confirm after every Voidlight Convergence reset.
  • Burning Heart deals constant passive fire damage throughout — communicate incoming spikes to healers during high-pressure moments.
  • On Death Drop (Belo'ren crashes at 0 HP), use Warlock Gateway immediately to reset position for the Rebirth phase. Pre-place the Gateway before the pull.
✚ Healer
  • Ashen Benediction applies a stacking 10% healing reduction per egg cycle — healing becomes progressively harder each resurrection. Plan cooldown usage for later cycles specifically.
  • Burning Heart ramps up significantly during egg burn phases — pre-ramp healing before each Rebirth, do not react after it starts.
  • Heroism/Bloodlust on egg burn phases, not during the phoenix phase — the Rebirth window is the actual damage check of the fight.
  • Ember add puddles are permanent and expand over time — keep track of safe space and heal around it as the arena shrinks.
⚔ DPS
  • Strictly follow color assignments — only absorb Light or Void mechanics that match your assigned Feather. Wrong-color absorption applies a 20-second damage debuff.
  • Light/Void Dive: soak circles spawn Ember adds and create permanent expanding puddles. Position soak circles on designated markers or the puddles deny arena space permanently.
  • Kill Ember adds before they die naturally — dead Embers become Ember Rebirth eggs that revive after 15 seconds if not destroyed. Prioritize eggs immediately.
  • Light/Void Eruption: only players of the matching color can successfully interrupt it — assign color-specific interrupters before the pull.
  • Radiant Echoes: moving orbs that create gaps when popped — only pop them if you are the correct matching color. Wrong color = 20-second damage-taken debuff.
  • Use Heroism/Bloodlust during the Rebirth egg burn phase — that is when the boss is stationary and most vulnerable.
🛡 Tank
  • Guardian's Edict fires faster and the stacking damage buff from missed cones applies more aggressively — zero tolerance for misabsorption on Heroic.
  • Death Drop knockback is significantly harder — Warlock Gateway is effectively required to handle repositioning, not just recommended.
  • Voidlight Convergence reassigns Feathers more frequently — both tanks must confirm color re-assignments after every reset, not just the first.
  • Burning Heart during egg phases ramps harder — pop a defensive pre-emptively before each Rebirth, not after taking damage.
✚ Healer
  • Ashen Benediction healing reduction accumulates faster per cycle — later egg phases are significantly harder. Plan and save major cooldowns for cycles 3+ specifically.
  • Burning Heart spike during egg phases is more extreme — pre-ramp cooldowns before Rebirth, do not react to the spike after it lands.
  • Ember adds spawn in higher volume — puddle spread happens faster, shrinking the safe area more aggressively each cycle.
  • Voidlight Convergence resets happen more frequently — dispel assignments must be re-communicated and ready every phase.
⚔ DPS
  • Two Ember adds spawn simultaneously from later Dives — Ember Rebirth egg pressure doubles. Assign add kill priority before the pull.
  • Eggs must be destroyed faster — egg revival window is effectively shorter due to increased Ember spawn overlap in later cycles.
  • Wrong-color Radiant Echo absorption on Heroic applies a longer and more punishing debuff — zero improvisation on echo assignments.
  • Light/Void Eruption interrupt windows are tighter — color-specific interrupters must be staged and ready at all times, not reactively assigned.
🛡 Tank
  • Guardian's Edict timing becomes irregular on Mythic — cannot be predicted by cast order alone. Both tanks must watch for visual and audio cues rather than counting casts.
  • Missing any cone on Mythic accelerates the damage buff exponentially — even a single missed absorption can end the attempt within the same cycle.
  • Death Drop requires Warlock Gateway AND tank-specific positioning pre-agreement — improvised Gateway placement causes knockback deaths.
✚ Healer
  • Ashen Benediction stacks faster and healing reduction is steeper — plan the full cooldown roster mapped to expected cycle count before the pull. Later cycles may be nearly unhealable without perfect cooldown timing.
  • Ember puddle spread is aggressive — call safe healing positions proactively as the arena shrinks each cycle.
  • Keep a cooldown available at all times for surprise Guardian's Edict misses — the damage buff spike from a missed cone is lethal without mitigation.
⚔ DPS
  • Ember Rebirth eggs on Mythic spawn with a brief invulnerability window — burst them immediately the moment it expires. Do not preemptively dump cooldowns before the window ends.
  • Radiant Echo wrong-color absorption on Mythic is near-lethal — pre-assign echo responsibilities strictly by Feather color. No freelancing.
  • Heroism/Bloodlust timing is critical — coordinate with healers to ensure it lands at an egg phase where healing reduction is manageable, not at peak Ashen Benediction stacks.
Boss 2 of 2 — Season Finale

Midnight Falls

The hardest encounter of Midnight Season 1. L'ura, a corrupted Naaru, fights across three distinct stages with two intermissions. Stage 1 centers on a musical rune puzzle and crystal management. The intermission pulls the entire raid inside the Darkwell. Stage 3 is a movement-and-DPS race against stacking Darkness. Come prepared with a Death's Dirge caller and a crystal management plan.

🛡 Tank
  • Heaven's Lance: stacking Impaled debuff (50% increased damage taken per stack) — swap tanks frequently before stacks become lethal. Do not wait for the co-tank to call it; watch the debuff yourself.
  • Position L'ura away from The Darkwell beam at all times — players caught in it are killed instantly.
  • Safeguard Matrices revolve around L'ura, each reducing her damage taken by 33% (up to 3 active) — keep L'ura positioned to allow DPS line of sight to break them. Communicate matrix count to DPS.
  • During the Total Eclipse intermission, all players are pulled into the Darkwell — stack with the group and survive the Eclipsed healing absorb. No tanking mechanics during this phase, focus on positioning.
✚ Healer
  • Dusk Crystals fade and begin taking increasing Shadow damage — convert them into Dawn Crystals by healing them. Dawn Crystals generate a Torchbearer aura (12-yard damage reduction for nearby players). This is a primary raid survival mechanic, not optional.
  • Midnight Crystals are DPS priority and detonate for heavy raid-wide damage if not destroyed — call them out immediately when they appear so DPS can respond.
  • Shattered Sky: 25-second Shadow damage rain — maintain sustained throughput throughout this window without dropping off.
  • During the Total Eclipse intermission: all players receive the Eclipsed healing absorb debuff — pour throughput into breaking the absorb before players die. This is your most intensive healing window.
  • In Stage 3: Darkness stacks reduce movement speed and deal escalating damage — this is the enrage equivalent. Map your final cooldowns to this stage before the pull.
⚔ DPS
  • Death's Dirge: L'ura marks players with musical note runes, then a laser activates each in sequence. Correct order = localized damage. Wrong order = 5 seconds of raid-wide Cosmic damage. Designate one caller before the pull to announce the sequence every time.
  • Destroy Midnight Crystals immediately — they detonate for heavy raid-wide damage if ignored. Always top priority over boss damage.
  • Break Safeguard Matrices orbiting L'ura — each one reduces her damage taken by 33%. With 3 active she takes 99% less damage. Break them to restore full damage.
  • Heaven's Glaives: whirling blades ricocheting around the arena — constant movement required. Do not tunnel boss and stand still during this mechanic.
  • In Stage 2 (Dark Reactor inside the Darkwell): use Galvanize beams on Void Cores. Prevent Core Harvest — if L'ura absorbs cores she regenerates energy. Cores are the add-equivalent of this stage.
  • Use Heroism/Bloodlust at the start of Stage 3 when Darkness stacks begin ramping — this is the enrage phase and the fight must be killed quickly.
🛡 Tank
  • Heaven's Lance stacks apply faster on Heroic — tank swaps must happen earlier. Do not wait for the co-tank to call — swap proactively at lower stack counts than on Normal.
  • Safeguard Matrices are harder to break — assign dedicated matrix breakers and keep L'ura positioned consistently to maintain DPS line of sight on them.
  • The Darkwell beam hitbox is wider on Heroic — give The Darkwell significantly more clearance when positioning L'ura.
✚ Healer
  • Death's Dirge melody complexity increases each cycle — wrong-order detonations are more likely as the fight progresses. Keep Shattered Sky cooldowns available for error recovery.
  • Tears of L'ura spawn from crystals on Heroic — additional movement hazards during crystal management. Healing while moving is more demanding; pre-ramp before crystal phases.
  • Midnight Crystals detonate for significantly more damage on Heroic — one missed crystal can cascade into a wipe. Call them out immediately and firmly.
  • Eclipsed absorb during the intermission is larger — more throughput is required to break through it. Major cooldown during the intermission is mandatory on Heroic.
⚔ DPS
  • Death's Dirge melody complexity increases each cycle on Heroic — later iterations of the mechanic require faster reaction. Practice the Dirge order early and build habit.
  • Tears of L'ura create additional movement hazards during crystal phases — plan movement paths around both Tears and Glaive patterns simultaneously.
  • Safeguard Matrices on Heroic require dedicated breakers — assign 2 DPS to matrix priority. Do not rely on incidental damage.
  • Stage 2 Void Core management is more demanding — Galvanize beam coordination requires pre-assigned beam users. Improvised assignment in Stage 2 causes Core Harvest.
🛡 Tank
  • Heaven's Lance Impaled stacks apply at a significantly higher rate on Mythic — both tanks must communicate every swap and have defensives pre-staged before taunting.
  • The Darkwell beam rotates on Mythic — L'ura positioning requires constant adjustment. Coordinate repositions with DPS to avoid interrupting matrix-breaking.
✚ Healer
  • Severance splits players across alternate dimensions — healers must cover their assigned dimension and cannot rely on cross-dimension support. Pre-assign healer-per-group before the pull.
  • Grim Symphony / Death's Requiem layer additional escalation mechanics on top of Death's Dirge — healing cooldown usage must be mapped to the full fight timeline including these Mythic-specific windows.
  • Darkness stacks in Stage 3 accumulate significantly faster — the final phase is extremely compressed. Heroism timing must be precise to the second.
⚔ DPS
  • Severance splits players across alternate dimensions on Mythic — coordinate which players go where before the pull. DPS must maintain crystal and Midnight Crystal management in both dimensions simultaneously.
  • Grim Symphony / Death's Requiem: Mythic-exclusive escalation added on top of existing Death's Dirge complexity. The Dirge caller must handle both systems without confusion — use callout software and WeakAuras.
  • Darkness stacks in Stage 3 accumulate far faster — use Heroism/Bloodlust at the precise start of Stage 3 to kill L'ura before the enrage becomes unbeatable.
  • Every major DPS cooldown must be held for Stage 3 — nothing used during Stage 1 or 2 unless absolutely necessary for crystal management emergencies.
