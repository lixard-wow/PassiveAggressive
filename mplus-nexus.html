Nexus-Point Xenas

3 Bosses • Voidstorm • An ethereal fortress pulsing with unstable Void energy after Lothraxion's assault destabilized the Corespark Engine. Three distinct wings: ethereals west, void east, Light north.

Tactyks Guide — Nexus-Point Xenas

⚔ Trash Pull Notes

📶 Priority Targets
  • Shadowguard Arcanist — Arcane Torrent nukes the whole party. Kill or interrupt before anything else.
  • Void Tendril — pulses AoE shadow damage to all players every second while alive. Kill immediately on spawn; do not let it channel.
  • Lightscarred Remnant — applies Null Zone on random players, reducing healing received by 40%. Kill before healers lose effectiveness.
  • Corespark Remnant — stacks a damage-taken debuff on the tank via Resonating Pulse. Kill fast or interrupt every cast.
⚡ Must Kick
  • Arcane Torrent (Shadowguard Arcanist) — party-wide arcane burst. Never let this cast.
  • Resonating Pulse (Corespark Remnant) — stacks a damage amplification debuff on the tank with every uninterrupted cast.
  • Void Bolt Chain (Void Stalker) — chains between nearby players and bounces up to 5 times. Interrupt to cut the chain immediately.
  • Light Mend (Lightscarred Remnant) — heals itself and nearby allies; undoes significant damage if not kicked.
🔒 CC Targets
  • Shadowguard Arcanists — Polymorph (humanoid), Hex. CC the second one when pulling double Arcanist packs.
  • Void Stalkers — Imprison (Demon Hunter), Hex (Shaman/Evoker).
  • Lightscarred Remnants — Polymorph (humanoid); CC if you cannot kill them before Null Zone applies.
🔮 Purge / Soothe
  • Void Shield (various void creatures) — Spellsteal (Mage), Purge (Shaman), Devour Magic (Warlock), or Naturalize (Evoker) strips the absorb before it soaks your damage.
  • Arcane Empowerment (Shadowguard Arcanist self-buff) — Purge or Spellsteal immediately when it appears.
Boss 1 of 3

Chief Corewright Kasreth

Leyline Arrays are lethal beams linking conduits across the room. Do not cross them. The player with Reflux Charge can destroy them — move to each array and clear as many as possible.

🛡 Tank
  • Position Kasreth near Leyline Arrays when a DPS has Reflux Charge — proximity maintains melee uptime while they clear beams.
  • At 100 energy, Corespark Detonation fires — dodge the impact zones and move out of Flux Collapse puddles immediately.
  • Manage knockback from Corespark Detonation — position to avoid being thrown into an active Leyline Array.
✚ Healer
  • Corespark Detonation is followed immediately by Sparkburn on the whole group (absorbs incoming heals) — time your healing output before it hits, not after.
  • Players crossing an active Leyline Array take lethal damage — communicate clearly if someone is about to cross.
  • Steady throughput between Detonations; Sparkburn windows are the hardest healing checks.
⚔ DPS
  • Never cross an active Leyline Array without a defensive cooldown — the damage is lethal.
  • When you receive Reflux Charge, immediately move to destroy as many Leyline Arrays as possible — clearing them creates safe movement lanes.
  • Sidestep Flux Collapse puddles and dodge Corespark Detonation impact zones.
Boss 2 of 3

Corewarden Nysarra

Nysarra enters at roughly 60% health. During Null Vanguard, adds spawn — kill them before Lightscar Flare stuns her for 18 seconds. The stun is a 300% damage vulnerability window.

🛡 Tank
  • Umbral Lash cleaves nearby players and applies Void Gash (50% increased damage taken on you) — position Nysarra so the cleave does not hit ranged.
  • Eclipsing Step repositions Nysarra behind a random target — reacquire threat and reposition immediately every time.
  • Pick up Dreadflail and Grand Nullifier adds during Null Vanguard and keep them separated — Dreadflail frontals are dangerous if adds overlap.
✚ Healer
  • Use your major cooldowns at the start of the 18-second Lightscar Flare stun — standing inside the Lightscar wound increases your healing output by 30%.
  • Manage Void Gash stacks on the tank — 50% increased damage taken makes tank busters lethal at high stack counts.
  • Dusk Frights cause fear effects during add phases — dispel immediately.
⚔ DPS
  • Kill Dreadflail and Grand Nullifier adds during Null Vanguard before the Lightscar Flare stun begins — the stun only happens after the adds are dead.
  • Kick: Grand Nullifier casts during Null Vanguard — interrupt immediately. On Mythic+: also interrupt Devour the Unworthy.
  • When Lightscar Flare stuns Nysarra, stack offensive cooldowns — she takes 300% increased damage for 18 seconds. This is your entire burn phase.
Boss 3 of 3 — Final

Lothraxion

At 100 energy, Divine Guile: Lothraxion disguises among his clones while channeling Core Overload. Identify the real one by the horns on his head and interrupt him. Wrong interrupt on Mythic+ = massive group damage.

🛡 Tank
  • Searing Rend is the primary tank damage ability — use defensive cooldowns. The DoT component stacks up on high keys.
  • Leave Radiant Scar ground patches away from group pathways — Fractured Images flicker through the room and contact causes them.
  • Call Divine Guile phases clearly — all DPS need to stop attacking and identify the real Lothraxion before any kick lands.
✚ Healer
  • Save a major cooldown for Core Exposure on Mythic+ — if the wrong image is interrupted, the group takes massive Holy damage and has 50% Holy vulnerability for 1 minute.
  • Manage split healing: tank takes Searing Rend DoT, ranged take Fractured Image contact damage. No downtime between these.
  • Use a healing cooldown at the start of each Divine Guile phase — image contact damage spikes until the real one is identified.
⚔ DPS
  • During Divine Guile: stop all DPS immediately, identify the real Lothraxion by the horns on his head, then interrupt Core Overload.
  • On Mythic+: interrupting the wrong image triggers Core Exposure — massive group damage plus 50% Holy vulnerability for 1 minute. Take a moment to be sure.
  • Between Divine Guile phases: burn hard. Avoid contact with all Fractured Images — each contact applies a stacking DoT.
