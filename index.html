Who We Are

We are a small community of Mythic+ players who prefer consistent groups instead of random pugs. Most of us have been playing together since Dragonflight and plan to continue pushing keys through Midnight and beyond.

The guild is designed for players who enjoy Mythic+ as their primary content. While we do run raids occasionally, our focus will always remain Mythic+.

Our goal is simple: have people online who want to run keys, improve each season, and keep the environment relaxed and drama free.
