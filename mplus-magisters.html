Magister's Terrace

4 Bosses • Isle of Quel'Danas • A reworked Midnight version of the classic sanctum. Arcane constructs, Void experiments, and a final boss that dissolves into clones.

Tactyks Guide — Magister's Terrace

⚔ Trash Pull Notes

📶 Priority Targets
  • Sunseeker Netherbinder — casts Spellbind, silencing a random player for several seconds, and buffs nearby allies' arcane damage. Kill or CC first.
  • Felblood Initiate — fixates on a random player and self-destructs on contact. Kill before it reaches anyone; do not let it close distance.
  • Sunseeker Astromage — drops Flamestrike AoEs that persist on the floor. Kill fast to limit floor coverage.
  • Blood Knight Legionnaire — charges into the group. Tank pulls toward a wall so the charge path is controlled.
⚡ Must Kick
  • Scorch (Sunseeker Astromage) — stacking fire damage on the tank. Interrupt every cast.
  • Spellbind (Sunseeker Netherbinder) — silences a player for several seconds; interrupt to prevent losing a healer mid-pull.
  • Corrupt (Felblood Elf) — spreading shadow debuff that chains to nearby players if not dispelled.
  • Arcane Lightning (various arcane casters) — high-damage bolt with a fast cast time. Rotate kicks across DPS.
🔒 CC Targets
  • Sunseeker Netherbinders — Polymorph (humanoid). Highest CC priority in any pull that includes them.
  • Sunseeker Astromages — Polymorph, Hex. CC to prevent Flamestrike from covering the floor.
  • Blood Knight Legionnaires — Repentance (Paladin), Polymorph, Hex.
  • Felblood Initiates — Polymorph to hold them in place; be ready to break CC and kill when the group is ready to focus.
🔮 Purge / Soothe
  • Arcane Empowerment (Sunseeker Netherbinder buff on allies) — Spellsteal (Mage), Purge (Shaman), Devour Magic (Warlock), Naturalize (Evoker).
  • Corrupt debuff on players — healer Dispel Magic or Purify removes the spreading shadow effect before it chains.
Boss 1 of 4

Arcanotron Custos

An arcane construct defending the terrace. Repulsing Slam knocks the tank back hard. Destroy Energy Orbs during Refueling Protocol before the boss absorbs them.

🛡 Tank
  • Repulsing Slam is the primary tank buster — use a major defensive every time. Position away from walls so the knockback does not pin you.
  • After the slam, close distance quickly — melee gap gives the boss a free cast window.
  • Arcane Residue pools accumulate across the floor — keep moving to avoid standing in them.
✚ Healer
  • Sustained arcane damage from Arcane Residue pools throughout — players who stand in them spike quickly.
  • Ethereal Shackles root players in place — dispel immediately or the rooted player takes full pool damage.
  • Save a cooldown for Refueling Protocol when the boss gains 20% reduced damage taken and damage spikes briefly.
⚔ DPS
  • Destroy Energy Orbs the instant they spawn during Refueling Protocol — each orb the boss absorbs empowers it significantly.
  • The 20% damage vulnerability window during Refueling Protocol is your burst phase — use cooldowns here.
  • Stay out of Arcane Residue puddles — they stack arcane damage and slow movement.
Boss 2 of 4

Seranel Sunlash

Vow of Silence silences everyone outside a Suppression Zone at 100 energy. Always know where the nearest zone is before energy caps.

🛡 Tank
  • Watch Seranel's energy bar — at 100 energy Vow of Silence silences all players outside Suppression Zones. Be inside one before it fires.
  • Hastening Ward buff dramatically increases his damage output — call it out for purgers immediately.
  • Position Seranel near a Suppression Zone so you can maintain melee while staying in range of the zone.
✚ Healer
  • Hastening Ward must be dispelled before tank buster windows — assign a Mage, Shaman, Evoker, or Warlock to purge on sight.
  • Vow of Silence silences you if you are outside a Suppression Zone — you cannot cast heals. Always be inside one when energy is high.
  • Runic Mark bounces between players and escalates damage — help coordinate group positioning in zones to reduce the chain.
⚔ DPS
  • Kick: Runic Mark cast — interrupt it to prevent the bounce chain from starting. Rotate all 3 DPS.
  • Purge: Hastening Ward off Seranel immediately — Mage (Spellsteal), Shaman (Purge), Evoker (Naturalize), Warlock (Devour Magic).
  • Before energy reaches 100, move inside a Suppression Zone — Vow of Silence silences anyone outside one for 8 seconds.
Boss 3 of 4

Gemellus

Replicates at 90% and 50% health. All copies share one health pool — damage any of them. Neural Link debuff clears by touching the correct clone.

🛡 Tank
  • Astral Grasp pulls you toward the boss — do not fight it. Moving with the pull avoids the Cosmic Radiation burst that punishes players who resist.
  • After each replication, reposition to keep all clones roughly together for group cleave.
  • Pick up fear-casting adds immediately — they fixate on healers and disrupt the whole fight if ignored.
✚ Healer
  • All clones share a health pool, meaning AoE mechanics hit all players simultaneously — position for group-wide events.
  • Fear-casting adds deal sustained damage and must be killed quickly — maintain healing pressure while adds are active.
  • Neural Link debuff on a player clears when they approach the correct clone — dispelling it prematurely spreads the effect.
⚔ DPS
  • Cleave all clones together — they share one health pool so any damage counts regardless of target.
  • Kill fear-casting adds immediately on spawn — assign one DPS to pick them up and burn them.
  • Interrupt: fear-casting adds are the primary interrupt target. Rotate kicks across the adds while maintaining boss pressure.
Boss 4 of 4 — Final

Degentrius

Unstable Void Essence floats across the arena and must be absorbed. If it lands unabsorbed, Null Bomb hits the entire group. Tank soaks Null Bomb with a defensive cooldown.

🛡 Tank
  • Null Bomb soak: when Unstable Void Essence lands and is not absorbed by the group, you must soak the resulting explosion — use a major defensive cooldown every time.
  • Track where Unstable Void Essence is floating and position your group beneath its landing zone when possible to share the soak.
  • Defensives on cooldown — Null Bomb detonations are frequent and stack up quickly on high keys.
✚ Healer
  • Devouring Entropy is applied to 2 players — only dispel when those players are away from the rest of the group. The dispel spawns Entropy Orbs that uncorrupted players must avoid.
  • Coordinate dispel timing with the afflicted players — ask them to move before you dispel.
  • Maintain throughput during Null Bomb phases; the tank is taking heavy burst from each soak.
⚔ DPS
  • Stay aware of Unstable Void Essence position — absorb it by stepping into its landing zone. Group absorption reduces Null Bomb damage.
  • Avoid Entropy Orbs spawned from Devouring Entropy dispels — move away from affected players when dispel goes out.
  • Coordinate with the tank on Null Bomb timing — call out when you see Essence about to land so they can prep their defensive.
