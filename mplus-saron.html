Pit of Saron

3 Bosses • Icecrown • The Scourge's frozen industrial forge. Features Permafrost stack management via boulders, one of the most chaotic kiting encounters in M+, and a straight DPS race against escalating stacks.

Tactyks Guide — Pit of Saron

⚔ Trash Pull Notes

📶 Priority Targets
  • Ymirjar Deathbringer — applies Empowerment aura to all nearby allies, increasing their damage by 35%. Kill first in every pull it appears in.
  • Ymirjar Flamebearer — Fire Nova hits all players simultaneously. Kill or interrupt before it casts.
  • Pallid Horror — high-damage caster with a fast Shadow Bolt. Kill before melee mobs when paired with them.
  • Skeletal Slaves (gauntlet) — numerous low-HP adds. AoE them immediately; they stack bleeds on the tank if ignored.
⚡ Must Kick
  • Fire Nova (Ymirjar Flamebearer) — simultaneous AoE fire hit on all players. Highest interrupt priority.
  • Empowerment (Ymirjar Deathbringer) — the buff cast itself can be interrupted before it applies to nearby allies.
  • Ice Shards (Ymirjar Huntress) — cone frost damage followed by a root. Kick to prevent the lockdown.
  • Shadow Bolt (Pallid Horror) — fast-cast high-damage bolt. Rotate DPS interrupts; this fires frequently.
🔒 CC Targets
  • Ymirjar Deathbringers — Shackle Undead (Priest) is most effective. Polymorph works if classified as humanoid.
  • Ymirjar Flamebearers — Shackle Undead (Priest), Freezing Trap (Hunter).
  • Ymirjar Huntresses — Shackle Undead, Freezing Trap (Hunter), Hex. CC when pulling groups that also include a Deathbringer.
  • Pallid Horrors — Shackle Undead (Priest) or Hex. CC the second caster when facing double-caster pulls.
🔮 Purge / Soothe
  • Empowerment buff applied to allied mobs by the Deathbringer — Purge (Shaman), Spellsteal (Mage), Devour Magic (Warlock) strips it off empowered allies.
  • Frost Enrage (any enraged undead near the forge) — Soothe (Druid) or Naturalize (Evoker). Priority when a Deathbringer is already empowering the pack.
Boss 1 of 3

Forgemaster Garfrost

Throw Saronite drops ore chunks on 2 players. Destroy them with Orebreaker — only one per Glacial Overload cycle. Position surviving ore chunks along arena edges for use as line-of-sight cover during Glacial Overload.

🛡 Tank
  • When Throw Saronite drops an ore chunk, use Orebreaker to destroy it before Overload. Destroy only one per cycle — surviving ore chunks are your LoS cover for Glacial Overload.
  • Position ore chunks along arena edges. You need several surviving chunks spread around the room for future LoS.
  • Cryostomp applies a frost debuff (50% more frost damage taken) to 2 players — call it out and ensure they are behind an ore chunk before Glacial Overload fires.
✚ Healer
  • Siphoning Chill is a constant passive frost aura — keep everyone above comfortable HP throughout, not just during Overload.
  • Cryostomp-debuffed players take 50% more damage from Glacial Overload — watch their HP carefully and top them before Overload.
  • During boulder resets the tank moves to a new ore position — maintain heals through the repositioning.
⚔ DPS
  • Help position and track ore chunks — know where your nearest LoS chunk is before each Glacial Overload cast.
  • Move behind an ore chunk before Glacial Overload fires — standing in the open is lethal.
  • Dodge Throw Saronite impact zones. Standing in them applies Cryostomp-style frost debuffs to DPS as well.
Boss 2 of 3

Ick & Krick

Necrolink links their health pools — any damage to either counts toward both. Krick mounts Ick during fixate phase. Shades of Krick adds spawn during Shade Shift — cleave them with the boss.

🛡 Tank
  • During "Get 'em, Ick!" fixate phase, Krick mounts Ick and they fixate on you — kite around the arena while DPS burns them freely. Avoid Plague Explosion pools while kiting.
  • Place Blight Smash puddles along arena edges — they persist and fill the room if dropped in the center.
  • During Shade Shift, drag Ick toward the spawning Shades of Krick for efficient cleave.
✚ Healer
  • Shade Shift applies a group DoT simultaneously with add spawns — use a burst cooldown during each Shade Shift.
  • During the fixate kiting phase, the tank takes steady damage — maintain heals while moving.
  • Shadowbind channel from Shades of Krick can be dispelled — remove it immediately from any affected player.
⚔ DPS
  • Necrolink means both bosses share one HP pool — damage either one. Cleave both together whenever possible.
  • Kick: Death Bolt from Krick — highest priority interrupt. Rotate all 3 DPS. Also interrupt Shadowbind channels from Shades of Krick.
  • During the fixate phase, DPS the combined boss freely while the tank kites. Kill Shades of Krick during Shade Shift phases.
Boss 3 of 3 — Final

Scourgelord Tyrannus

Position non-tank players near bone piles so Rime Blast (from Rimefang above) freezes them. Frozen piles cannot animate during Army of the Dead. Scourgelord's Brand knocks the tank back — move immediately from the landing spot.

🛡 Tank
  • Scourgelord's Brand sequence: absorb the knockback, then move immediately from where you land — Scourgelord's Reckoning AoE follows to your landing position. Do not land near allies.
  • Use defensive cooldowns for Scourgelord's Brand — it applies a 200% Shadow damage vulnerability for 6 seconds.
  • Between Army of the Dead waves, consider positioning for cleave on Plaguespreader adds alongside the boss.
✚ Healer
  • Festering Pulse is a passive aura from each active Scourge Plaguespreader add — more adds alive means more passive group damage. Use burst cooldowns during large add waves.
  • Scourgelord's Brand on the tank has a 200% Shadow vulnerability window — heavy heals immediately after each Brand.
  • No priority dispels needed — manage through steady throughput during add phases.
⚔ DPS
  • During early phases, position away from the boss near bone piles — Rime Blast from Rimefang freezes the pile you are near. Aim to freeze 4 of 5 piles to minimize adds during Army of the Dead.
  • When Army of the Dead spawns Plaguespreaders: immediately kill them all. Kick: Plague Bolt from every Plaguespreader — maintain a rotation across all 3 DPS on all active adds.
  • Use Heroism/Lust at the start of the fight — front-loading DPS wins this encounter. Unholy Power stacks make Tyrannus progressively harder to kill the longer the fight goes.
