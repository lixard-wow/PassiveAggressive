Seat of the Triumvirate

4 Bosses • Mac'Aree, Argus • A draenei sacred city corrupted by the Void. Featuring void pool management, extensive dispel requirements, Void Tendrils, and L'ura as one of the most punishing final bosses in the rotation.

Tactyks Guide — Seat of the Triumvirate

⚔ Trash Pull Notes

📶 Priority Targets
  • Shadowguard Voidtouched — Void Bolt Storm hits the entire party. Kill or interrupt before touching anything else in the pull.
  • Shadowguard Nethermancer — drains the healer's mana with Mana Void. Kill immediately; a drained healer loses the pull.
  • Void Tendril — pulses AoE shadow damage to all players every second while alive. Kill on sight.
  • Dark Aberration — fixates on a random party member. Tank establishes aggro immediately; DPS kills before fixate runs its full duration.
⚡ Must Kick
  • Void Bolt Storm (Shadowguard Voidtouched) — party-wide shadow burst. Highest interrupt priority throughout the dungeon.
  • Mana Void (Shadowguard Nethermancer) — drains the healer to empty. Kick immediately; missing this can end the pull.
  • Shadow Word: Agony (void casters) — stacking damage on a single player. Rotate DPS kicks.
  • Void Cascade (Dark Aberration) — shadow chain that bounces between nearby players. Interrupt to cut the chain.
🔒 CC Targets
  • Shadowguard Voidtouched — Polymorph (humanoid), Hex. CC the second one when pulling multiple casters.
  • Shadowguard Nethermancers — Polymorph is the top CC priority. If no Mage, use Hex (Shaman/Evoker).
  • Dark Aberrations — Imprison (Demon Hunter), Hex. CC to prevent fixate on the healer.
  • Void Tendrils — cannot be CC'd; must be killed.
🔮 Purge / Soothe
  • Void Shield (void creature absorb buff) — Spellsteal (Mage), Purge (Shaman), Devour Magic (Warlock), Naturalize (Evoker). Strip before spending DPS on a shielded target.
  • Shadowguard Empowerment (Nethermancer self-buff) — Purge or Spellsteal removes the caster's damage increase.
Boss 1 of 4

Zuraal the Ascended

Decimate: Zuraal leaps at a random player. The targeted player moves away; the impact creates a void crater that persists all fight. Direct all leaps toward arena edges to preserve usable floor.

🛡 Tank
  • Position Zuraal near the arena center — you need room to aim Decimate impacts toward edges. Void craters are permanent.
  • Kick: Void Slash — rotate with DPS to maintain consistent interrupts throughout.
  • Pick up Coalesced Void adds immediately — they walk toward Zuraal and empower him if they reach him.
✚ Healer
  • Oozing Slam applies a DoT to anyone hit — position away from slam zones and heal DoT players promptly.
  • Use cooldowns when Coalesced Void adds are active alongside boss mechanics — it is the highest simultaneous damage window.
  • Void Tendrils root random players — dispel immediately. Rooted players take amplified damage.
⚔ DPS
  • Kill Coalesced Void adds before they reach Zuraal — use hard CC (Freezing Trap, stuns) to slow them if needed. Add priority over boss.
  • When Decimate targets you, move away from your current position — the impact crater spawns where you were standing. Aim it toward an edge.
  • Avoid void craters from previous Decimates — they accumulate and fill the room over time.
Boss 2 of 4

Saprish

Saprish fights with two pets. All three share one health pool — cleave all three together. Shadewing's Dread Screech requires two simultaneous interrupts. Overload is the group burst window.

🛡 Tank
  • Keep Saprish and both pets stacked together for maximum cleave efficiency. All three share a health pool.
  • When Saprish vanishes via Shadow Stalk, watch the reappearance indicator and taunt immediately on return.
  • Pick up Shadow Hound adds via AoE threat — they fixate on healers if not taunted.
✚ Healer
  • Saprish's Overload is a group-wide burst — use a major cooldown before it hits.
  • Voidtouched debuff drains healing received on the afflicted player — dispel it immediately.
  • Multiple Shadow Hound adds and bosses create a chaotic damage environment — maintain steady throughput rather than saving cooldowns.
⚔ DPS
  • Cleave all three targets (Saprish + 2 pets) — they share one HP pool so simultaneous damage is maximally efficient.
  • Kick: Shadewing's Dread Screech requires 2 simultaneous interrupts — assign 2 DPS to this and coordinate timing.
  • Kill Shadow Hound adds quickly — each uncontrolled hound fixates the healer and applies bleed stacks.
Boss 3 of 4

Viceroy Nezhar

Umbral Tentacles spawn in sets of 5 and channel Mind Flay. All 5 must die before Collapsing Void or they all fire simultaneously. This is a hard DPS check every wave.

🛡 Tank
  • Drag Nezhar away from gate spawn positions — Umbral Waves from Gates of the Abyss need clear travel paths.
  • Kick: Mind Blast — maintain a consistent rotation. Nezhar has no cooldown on it.
  • During Collapsing Void, move under the boss — this avoids Umbral Waves from the gates.
✚ Healer
  • Save major cooldowns for each Umbral Tentacle wave — 5 Mind Flays simultaneously is the peak damage window if any tentacle survives.
  • Manage knockback from Nezhar — communicate if you are about to be thrown into a gate or wave.
  • Echoes of Oblivion create zones where heals partially fail — reposition out of them immediately.
⚔ DPS
  • Kill all 5 Umbral Tentacles before Collapsing Void fires — surviving tentacles all Mind Flay simultaneously. Use offensive cooldowns for every tentacle wave.
  • Kick: Mind Blast on Nezhar — help the tank maintain the rotation.
  • During Collapsing Void, move under the boss to avoid Umbral Waves. Once the wave passes, return to your normal position.
Boss 4 of 4 — Final

L'ura

A fallen Naaru. Notes of Despair deal constant unavoidable group damage throughout. Abyssal Lance stacks on the tank — defensive at 3 stacks every time. Siphon Void is your offensive cooldown window.

🛡 Tank
  • Abyssal Lance stacks on you — use a major defensive at 3 stacks without exception. Externals (Ironbark, Blessing of Protection) are valuable here on high keys.
  • Position in the Path of the Void Surge when it spawns — this soak mechanic requires tank participation.
  • Face L'ura away from Void Surge paths when soaks are not active — her cleave pattern changes.
✚ Healer
  • Notes of Despair deal constant, unavoidable group damage that never stops — spread healing cooldowns evenly throughout the fight. Do not save them all for later.
  • Grim Chorus and Disintegrate are additional spike windows on top of the baseline damage — always have something ready.
  • Void Madness applies a mind-control debuff — dispel before the affected player acts against the group.
⚔ DPS
  • During Siphon Void debuff windows, use all offensive cooldowns — this is your burst phase.
  • Kick: Void Madness — one uninterrupted cast mind-controls a player during a critical mechanic. Any DPS with an interrupt should be on this.
  • Dodge Disintegrate rotating beams and Grim Chorus ground circles. Keep Void Orbs from reaching L'ura — intercept them by running through their path.
